About Me

Madison Dragon

I am a New York based Graphic Design student currently studying at the Fashion Institute of Technology. I grew up backstage of the Arizona Herberger Theater Center. From the age of three, I was helping build sets and props, and I was navigating the labyrinthian halls of a professional sceneshop. That strange environment molded me into the artist I am today. I strive in loud, fast paced environments, and I enjoy being a part of a team.
